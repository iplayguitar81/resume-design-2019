Lilac City Dog Walking & Care offers a variety of services from pet-sitting, dog-walking, and even cat-sitting care. Below is a price guide for these services. Please note that extended care options are negotiable and are based upon work needed, availability, and overnight stays cannot always be accommodated. Multiple pets are not a problem and provided there are no more than 3, can usually be walked altogether. This, of course, depends upon leash behavior and/or size and the walker's physical ability to safely manage multiple pets. Lilac City Dog Walking & Care does not walk pets off-leash, but will play off-leash in a fenced yard that is properly secure.

$9 for a 30 to 40-minute walk for one dog

$40 for a 5-day work-week daily walk

$16 for weekends (Saturday & Sunday) daily walk

More than one dog? Just $13 for a 30 to 40-minute walk (Weekly rates negotiable! Please call to discuss rates for multiple pet weekly walks).

Extended care options also available for clients in need of help while out of town. The rates are generally between $13 and $20 and depend on the amount of home and pet maintenance needed while you are away.

Discounts can be arranged for seniors and I can gladly visit retirement communities for help with walking dogs and caring for cats.

Credit cards accepted as well as PayPal!
